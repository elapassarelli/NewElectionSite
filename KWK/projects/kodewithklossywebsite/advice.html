If you are attending the Los Angeles program, our first piece of advice to you is to GET EXCITED! This will definitely be the highlight of your summer. Should you find yourself feeling a little nervous, don't worry! You are in great hands with the wonderful teachers Jeff and Taylor, along with the TAs, who will make you feel completely at home! There are plenty of chances to get to know each other throughout the days, and the environment is completely supportive of every student, whether you're coming in with experience or none at all!

If you're worried about meeting new people, push yourself a little out of your comfort zone! If everyone in the groupchat is going for coffee, go for coffee! It's a great opportunity to get to know the girls you are going to be with for the next two-weeks, and they will likely become life-long friends.

If no one has initiated anything, the other girls are probably just as worried as you! See if everyone wants to hang out outside of class, it couldn't hurt!

For day to day, the best thing you can do is make sure you get a a good night's sleep and eat a good breakfast so that you are feeling your best throughout the day! And get excited or lunch, because the food is seriously SO good!
