Kode with Klossy is a two week summer program put on by supermodel turned coder, Karlie Kloss. The program is designed to encourage and empower young girls to learn computer science, a field that is predominantly male. The course teaches the language Ruby over an advance two-week curriculum in three different locations, St.Louis, NYC, and LA.

What is in a typical day: We start every morning with a coding challenge from the day before. At 9:00, Jeff and Taylor typically begin a lecture on a new subject, like Ruby, HTML, or CSS. We break for lunch and in the afternoon we have time for labs and bigger projects. Throughout the day, we take breaks to play games and bond. There are always good snacks to help fuel our brains!
